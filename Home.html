Hello World!_

Hello World and Welcome to my awesome website! This website holds all the projects I have made and anything I'm really passionate about. Here you will find (In due time coding is hard) a working game of pong, a gallery of funny photoshopped pictures, all my coding projects and more! This site is a love-letter to the internet of old (or web 1.0), a place which I never grew up with but, in comparison to what we currently have, it is truly a dream of mine. I absolutely despise the sleek minimalist advertisment filled slop that I adore the decentralized and anonymous nature, the wacky and colorful UIs, but, most importantly, I adore the community behind the websites. That community makes even the worst website seem like gold compared to a soulless corporate masterpiece. Brayden's Innovations is a culmination of everything that makes me who I am and I hope you can find something you like here or at least you will find yourself entertained here.
