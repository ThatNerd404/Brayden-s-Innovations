Welcome!

Hello World and Welcome to my awesome website! This website holds all the projects I have made and anything I'm really passionate about. Here you will find (In due time coding is hard) a working game of pong, a gallery of funny photoshopped pictures, all my coding projects and more! This site is a love-letter to the internet of old (or web 1.0), a place which I never grew up with but, in comparison to what we currently have which is sleek and advertisement filled slop, it is truly a dream of mine. I adore the decentralized and anonymous nature, the wacky and colorful UIs, but, most importantly, I adore the community behind the websites. That community makes even the worst website seem like gold compared to a soulless corporate masterpiece. Brayden's Innovations is a culmination of everything that makes me who I am and I hope you can find something you like here or at least you will find yourself entertained here.

Whoami?

I am Brayden! A beginner programmer hoping to get into the software development field and show what I can do! I have been interested in computers from a young age and have only recently come across the wonderful world of web development! I see the beauty and wonder in the creation of websites and being frank I believe everyone should have one. As a younger guy myself, I have only grown around the internet run and controlled by big congolmerates but I long for the decentralized internet of old. Which is why I created this website! I hope I can inspire at least one person to take part in the wonderful act of creation of something as beautiful as a website! To anyone reading this know that you are loved and that everyday is a gift. Go out in the world and do something, I implore you!

You should expect a smooth, beautiful UI and a fun experince! I also plan on giving this website regular updates so be ready to see changes to the website each time you visit!
