Whoami?

I am Brayden! A beginner programmer hoping to get into the software development field and show what I can do! I have been interested in computers from a young age and have only recently come across the wonderful world of web development! I see the beauty and wonder in the creation of websites and being frank I believe everyone should have one. As a younger guy myself, I have only grown around the internet run and controlled by big congolmerates but I long for the decentralized internet of old. Which is why I created this website! I hope I can inspire at least one person to take part in the wonderful act of creation of something as beautiful as a website!
