Favorite song: OH! TENGO SUERTE
Favorite album: AN INSATIABLE HIGH
Overall rating: 10/10

Masayoshi Takanaka is a Japananese man born in Tokyo, 1953. Mr.Takanaka began his solo career in music in 1976 with his debut album, "SEYCHELLES". Mr.Takanaka does jazz, jazz fusion, pop and rock. This wide array of genres make a very versatile artist able to cater to a plethora of tastes. Mr.Takanaka creates music unlike any other that shaped Japanese and American music forever. Mr.Takanaka's sound can only be described as the noise of relaxtion and chillness. I implore you to take a listen to Mr.Takanaka's extensive discography.
