Masayoshi Takanaka

Masayoshi Takanaka is a Japananese man born in Tokyo, 1953. Mr.Takanaka began his solo career in music in 1976 with his debut album, "SEYCHELLES". Mr.Takanaka music is a blend of jazz fusion and surf rock. This wide array of genres make a very him versatile artist able to cater to a plethora of tastes. Mr.Takanaka creates music unlike any other that shaped Japanese and American music forever. Mr.Takanaka's sound can only be described as the noise of relaxtion and chillness. I implore you to take a listen to Mr.Takanaka's extensive discography for an experince you will not regret. P.S Masayoshi Takanaka is very obviously not a small artist. Furthermore he's a phenomenon in Japan. Having said that, he is one of my most treasured artists so I am still going to place him here.

Miscellaneous

Miscellaneous is an 18 year-old American rapper from Little Rock, Arkansas. Miscellaneous began making music in 2020 with his debut single, "Comeback". Miscellaneous's music is mostly rap but with inspiration and tiny bits taken from differnet genres. Miscellaneous's sound can be described as an older take on rap, reminisent of an age of Tupac and Biggie Smalls. To any rap fan out there, Miscellaneous is a wonderful artist to try out and a nice bridge into the more niche side of rap.

INOHA

INOHA is an up-and-coming band composed of Keanu Bicol, Ricky Juarez, Christian Young, and Gavin Gonzalez. INOHA began with its debut album, "ALOHA INOHA". This album being a beautiful "hello" to an amazing band. INOHA's music is a unique take on indie rock and surf rock. INOHA's sound is a unique blend of each of the member's strengths turning into a harmony of exciting and unique rock music. It is so exciting to see new talent in the world and you should check them out today!
