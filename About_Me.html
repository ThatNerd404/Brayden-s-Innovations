Meet the Webmaster!
I am Brayden, your lovely webmaster! I'm infatuated with the internet of old and the ecosystem that it had. This site is a love-letter to the internet of old (or web 1.0, yesterweb, retro web, indie web etc). Although I did not grow up with the old web I have fallen in love with its decentralized and anonymous nature, the wacky, colorful, and creative UIs, but most importantly I adore the community behind these websites. That community makes even the worst website seem like gold compared to a soulless corporate masterpiece. Brayden's Innovations is a culmination of everything that makes me who I am and I hope you can find something that brightens your day here!
